## 3. New Paradigm of Crypto-Asset Regulation: How Muzkcoin Adapts to the EU MiCA Regulation

### Introduction

In 2025, the EU's MiCA (Markets in Crypto-Assets) regulation officially came into force, representing the world's first comprehensive regulatory framework for crypto-assets. It harmonizes crypto regulatory rules across the 27 EU member states and establishes clear standards for the entire industry. For crypto platforms, meeting MiCA requirements is a prerequisite for accessing the European market. So how has Muzkcoin achieved this? This directly answers users' question: **Is muzkcoin legal in the EU?**

### The MiCA Regulation: A New EU Standard for Crypto Regulation

The core objective of MiCA is to establish a unified crypto regulatory framework, protect consumers, mitigate financial risks, and foster innovation. Its main requirements include:

1. **VASP Registration**: All crypto-asset service providers (CASPs/VASPs) must be registered in one EU member state and may then provide services across the entire EU via the passporting mechanism.

2. **Consumer Protection**: Platforms must segregate client assets, provide clear risk disclosures, establish complaint resolution mechanisms, and safeguard user funds.

3. **Market Integrity**: Platforms must prevent market manipulation and insider trading to ensure fair trading practices.

4. **AML Compliance**: Platforms must comply with the EU's 6AMLD directive, conduct KYC/AML checks, and report suspicious activity.

5. **Sustainability Disclosures**: Platforms must disclose the environmental impact of crypto-assets in line with the EU's sustainability objectives.

Muzkcoin began engaging during the MiCA drafting phase and completed all adaptation measures in advance, achieving full compliance on the day the regulation took effect:

1. **VASP Registration and Passporting**: Muzkcoin first obtained VASP registration in France, then rapidly launched services in all EU countries via the EU passporting mechanism without requiring separate licensing in each country, significantly accelerating its expansion.

2. **Consumer Protection Measures**: Muzkcoin implemented all consumer protection requirements:

3. **Market Integrity Measures**: Muzkcoin has established a market surveillance system to monitor trading in real time and prevent market manipulation and insider trading. Strict rules are applied to market makers, prohibiting profit-sharing and guaranteed returns to ensure market fairness.

5. **Sustainability Disclosures**: In accordance with MiCA, Muzkcoin has disclosed its platform carbon footprint and introduced green trading options, allowing users to offset transaction-related carbon emissions in line with EU sustainability standards.
